About

Hello, my name is Evan Whitmer. I am a senior computer science major hoping to hone my skills in a variety of languages that will help me land a career after graduation. I am currently studying in a number of web development classes that I expect to use as I peruse a position as a full-stack developer. My current class schedule has me learning bash commands, scripts, and use standards, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Web APIs, project management in a agile style, and Jira.

Originally, growing up I had a great interest in computers and mathematics, which is why while deciding on a major I new that something that has me working in a team environment behind a computer screen was fit for me. After taking numerous math classes at Franciscan University, I knew I never wanted to use it more than I must. Thus, I tried to through myself into the deep end of learning Python, HTML, and CSS. I found that they were easy to understand conceptually and if I ever ran into a problem, there wasn’t a simple Google search that couldn’t solve it.

As I progress through my final year, learning, attending classes, and having fun at chess or board game club I hope that the skills I’ve learned will be enough as I start search for jobs and where life will lead me next.
