About

Hello, my name is Evan Whitmer. I am a senior computer science major hoping to hone my skills in a variety of languages that will help me land a career after graduation. I am currently studying in a number of web development classes that I expect to use as I pursue a position as a full-stack developer. My current class schedule has me learning bash commands, scripts, and usage standards, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Web APIs, project management in an agile style, and Jira.

Originally, growing up I had a great interest in computers and mathematics, which is why while deciding on a major I knew that something that has me working in a team environment behind a computer screen was fit for me. After taking numerous math classes at Franciscan University, I knew I never wanted to use it more than I must. Thus, I tried to throw myself into the deep end of learning Python, HTML, and CSS. I found that they were easy to understand conceptually, and if I ever ran into a problem, there wasn’t a simple Google search that couldn’t solve it.

As I progress through my final year, learning, attending classes, and having fun at chess or board game club, I hope that the skills I’ve learned will be enough as I start searching for jobs and where life will lead me next.
